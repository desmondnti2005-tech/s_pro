Our Story

A Legacy of Excellence

Founded in 2010 under the patronage of St. Anthony of Padua, our school has been a beacon of intellectual rigor and moral character for over six decades. We believe that true education encompasses the mind, the heart, and the spirit.

Our dedicated faculty, world-class facilities, and vibrant community create an environment where every student is challenged to discover their potential and inspired to pursue greatness with courage and compassion.

Submit Inquiry

02

Entrance Assessment

All applicants complete our entrance assessment in English, Mathematics, and critical reasoning.

03

Selected candidates and their parents are invited for a formal interview with our admissions team.
